Lemonade Stand

An ode to the body of work that musically and spiritually moved women and people everywhere.
As women blaze new trails it's important we take a look inside the
struggles, triumphs, and thoughts of those who birthed us all.

For me, feminism is a movement for which the end goal is to make itself no longer needed.

-Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie,
Author and self-proclaimed feminist

I wouldn't ask anyone to do anything I wouldn't do myself.

-Indra Nooyi,
CEO of PepsiCo

I don't know if every artist feels like they should have to make work that is about social issues, but I think they are missing out on a great opportunity if they aren't.

-Tatyana Fazlalizadeh,
Artist & Creator of Stop Telling Women to Smile

Let us pick up our books and our pens, they are our most powerful weapons.

-Malala Yousafzi,
Author, Survivor, & Girls Education Advocate
